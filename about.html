About Green Haven

Founded in 2022 by plant enthusiast Maungo Aone, Green Haven began as a small neighborhood plant shop with a big vision. What started as a passion project has blossomed into a beloved destination for plant lovers across the region.

We specialize exclusively in potted plants because we believe they're the perfect way to bring nature indoors. Each plant in our collection is carefully selected for its beauty, hardiness, and ability to thrive in home environments.

Our Mission

To make urban living greener by providing high-quality plants that are easy to care for, helping people connect with nature in their daily lives.

Our Values

Sustainability, quality, and education. We source responsibly and provide detailed care instructions with every plant.

Our Promise

Every plant is nurtured with care and comes with our personal guarantee. We're here to support your plant journey.
